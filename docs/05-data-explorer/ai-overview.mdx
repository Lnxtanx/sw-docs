# Resona AI

Resona AI is the agentic data assistant built into the Data Explorer. You describe what you want to know in plain English — Resona figures out which tables to query, runs the analysis, and returns the results as interactive charts, tables, and reports.

> Quick links: [Open Data Explorer](https://data-explorer.schemaweaver.vivekmind.com/) · [Data Explorer Overview](https://docs.schemaweaver.vivekmind.com/data-explorer/) · [SQL Editor](https://sql-editor.schemaweaver.vivekmind.com/)

![Resona AI panel open inside the Data Explorer with an active conversation and chart artifact](/images/data-explorer/resona-ai.png)

## What can Resona do inside Data Explorer?

### Explore and query your data

Ask questions without writing SQL. Resona automatically discovers your schema, constructs the right queries, and returns clean results.

> "Show me the top 10 customers by revenue this quarter"
> "How many orders were placed each day last month?"
> "@products list all items with fewer than 5 units in stock"

### Analyse trends and patterns

Resona has dedicated tools for time series, distributions, correlations, and anomaly detection. It picks the right analysis automatically based on what you ask.

> "Is there a seasonal pattern in our signups?"
> "Which columns in @events correlate most with conversion?"
> "Flag any unusual spikes in the @transactions table"

### Segment and compare

Break down data by cohorts, segments, or time periods. Compare behaviour between groups or track retention over time.

> "What's the 30-day retention for users who signed up in Q1 vs Q2?"
> "Compare average order value between mobile and desktop customers"
> "Show a funnel from signup → activation → first purchase"

### Generate reports and presentations

Resona can produce a full analysis report and export it as a PowerPoint deck or PDF, ready to share with stakeholders — without leaving the Data Explorer.

> "Summarise the key findings from today's analysis as a PDF"
> "Create a PowerPoint with the revenue trend and top product charts"

![Resona AI chat response showing an expanded data table artifact](/images/data-explorer/resona-ai-chat-expand-table.png)

## How do you access Resona AI?

Click the **Resona AI** button in the top-right of the Data Explorer, or the AI icon in the table sidebar. The AI panel opens alongside your data grid — both stay visible at the same time.

![Data Explorer interface showing the Resona AI button location in the header](/images/data-explorer/data-explorer.png)
