## What are AI Credits?

AI credits are the universal currency for using **Resona AI** across both the SQL Editor and the Data Explorer. 

- **Unified Pool:** Credits are shared across your entire workspace. A DDL edit in the SQL Editor and a chart generation in the Data Explorer draw from the same monthly pool.
- **Model Tiers:** Different models cost different amounts. Fast models (like GPT-4o mini) are inexpensive, while Powerful models (like Claude Opus) consume more credits per request.
- **Monthly Refresh:** Your credit balance resets at the start of every billing cycle.

## FAQ

### Is there a free trial?
We don't offer a traditional "timed" trial because our **Free plan is permanent**. You can use the full SQL Editor and 20-layer compiler forever without a credit card.

### Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. All paid plans are month-to-month. You can cancel at any time from your account settings, and you will retain access to your paid features until the end of your current billing period.

### Does Resona AI see my row-level data?
No. Resona AI only receives schema metadata (table/column names) and your questions. The generated SQL runs locally in your browser. Your private data never leaves your infrastructure.
